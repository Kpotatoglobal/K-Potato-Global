## 🧬 About

K-Potato Global is an integrated agricultural platform focused on developing climate-resilient potato varieties and sustainable seed potato systems.

Our mission is to improve global food security, increase farmer income, and build scalable agricultural systems through research, technology, and international collaboration.

---

## 🥔 Key Potato Varieties

- **Golden King** – High yield, processing excellence  
- **Happy King (Tongil)** – Widely adaptable, stable production  
- **Rose King** – Premium red skin variety  
- **Purple King** – Functional potato with high antioxidant properties  
- **Colored Functional Potatoes** – For health-oriented food markets  

---

## 🌱 Seed Potato System

We specialize in disease-free seed potato production:

- Tissue culture propagation  
- Net-based polytunnel systems  
- Virus control (aphid management)  
- 5-step IPM strategy  
- Seed multiplication model (40% sale / 60% seed retention)

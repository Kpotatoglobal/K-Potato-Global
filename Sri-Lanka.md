# 🇱🇰 Sri Lanka K-Potato Project

## Overview
This project focuses on establishing a self-sufficient seed potato production system in Sri Lanka.

## Key Activities
- Introduction of K-Potato varieties (Golden King, Happy King, Rose King)
- Field trials and adaptability testing
- Farmer training programs
- Polytunnel-based seed production systems

## Achievements
- Successful field validation in multiple regions
- Improved yield and quality compared to local varieties
- Strong interest from government and research institutes

## Future Plan
- Expansion to national seed system
- Establishment of centralized seed production facilities
- Scaling farmer adoption

## Collaboration
Open for collaboration with:
- Sri Lankan government
- Agricultural research institutes
- International development organizations
